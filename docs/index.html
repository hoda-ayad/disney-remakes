Prelim Concept - Does Disney Make Original Movies Anymore?

Dataset Description

Aggregates movies produced by labels owned by the Walt Disney Company including information about the “originality” of each. The goal is to study the remake phenomenon on a small scale to determine if Disney (and potentially other studios) is actually relying more heavily on established properties than before. This data (see csv) is manually categorized at different levels of originality.

Labeling scheme

  • O = Original
  • A = Adaptation
  • A* = Adaptation (Debatable)
  • S = Sequel
  • AS = Sequel of Adaptation
  • R = Reboot/Remake
  • N = NonFiction

NOTE: Limited to English-language fiction movies produced by one or more Disney-owned labels during Disney ownership. It will be important to define how much of a role the Disney label needs to have had in joint-produced films. This dataset can be expanded in the future to include other major film studios or TV shows.
